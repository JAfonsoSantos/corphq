Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 July 2026

CorpHQ is a free, fan-made companion tool for the game SpaceCraft. It is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Shiro Games. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights under the EU GDPR.

What we collect

  • Your email address — used only to send you a one-time login code (there are no passwords). We do not send marketing and never sell or share your email.
  • Data you enter — your corp, bases, deposits, production, chains and layouts. This is shared with members of your corp so everyone sees the same picture.
  • Anonymous usage analytics — aggregated, cookie-free page/visit counts via Cloudflare Web Analytics. This does not identify you and is not used for advertising.
  • Basic product telemetry — anonymous events (e.g. which screens are opened) and any feedback you submit through the in-app feedback button, to help us fix bugs and improve the tool.

Cookies & local storage

CorpHQ does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We store a single login session token in your browser's local storage so you don't have to sign in every time — this is strictly necessary for the service to work. Our analytics are cookie-free, so no consent banner is required.

Who processes your data

  • Supabase — authentication and database (your login and corp data).
  • Cloudflare — content delivery, security and cookie-free analytics.
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting.

Your rights (GDPR)

You can access, correct or delete your data at any time. In the app you can leave or (as owner) delete your corp, which removes the associated data. To request full deletion of your account and email, contact us and we will action it.

Retention & security

We keep your data while your account/corp is active. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and access is protected by row-level security. Anonymous analytics are retained by Cloudflare for a limited period only.

Contact

Data requests and privacy questions: joseafonsosantos@gmail.com, or use the in-app 🐞 feedback button.
